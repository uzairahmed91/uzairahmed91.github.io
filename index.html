Electrified village with DC Microgrid Solution

28 November 2021 | Changing Lives

With the fund collaboration from GIZ and our industrial partner Eco-energy, I led a team in an off grid village near Shujabaad region of Multan, electrifying twenty houses by installing in each a 500 Watts solar system capable of peer-to-peer sharing.

MOU signed for electrifying villages near Multan

3 April 2022 | Rural Empowerment

A new agreement was signed in between LUMS and Hema Verte for elctrifying off grid villages in the cotton belt of Multan in the coming August. This university-industrial collaboration will definitely change the dynamics of people living without electricity infrastructure.
